Air Cargo Services

Air freight transportation is a fast and efficient shipping method that is particularly valuable to businesses needing to get products to international customers quickly or stay on track with a just-in-time manufacturing model. The primary downside of air shipping is that the cost can be significantly higher than ocean freight forwarding. Fortunately, Affinity Trans Logistics can help lower this cost while also providing exceptional customer support.

We and our agents around the world have the same goal - to provide outstanding service and competitive pricing.

Affinity Trans has no interest in being a high-volume, one-model-fits-all provider. Our clients are not just numbers, and every shipment is given the individual attention to detail it requires. While many larger air freight forwarders are bound by consolidation schedules, we are able to offer greater flexibility for our client’s cargo through seamless quotation process, and team of industry experts specializing in Air Freight or Air Cargo Services.

Our dependable worldwide scheduled consolidations and door-to-door transportation services ensure shortened lead times, providing the security and reliability required by today's businesses.

Ocean Freight

Ocean Freight Services

Offering sea freight-forwarding services (LCL & FCL) from & to Through key gateway ports Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata - India

As a licensed Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC) for both Full Container Load (FCL) and Less Than Container Load (LCL) shipments, our ocean freight forwarding experience allow us to create the best shipping options for our clients, ensuring their cargo is managed cost-efficiently. Affinity Trans Logistics through its extensive network of agents across the world provide reliable and efficient NVOCC (non-vessel-operating common carrier) services for customers who look to export / import products from / to Indian ports. By leveraging our strong and long-term relationships with the global carriers “Affinity” offers you flexible, reliable and secure ocean freight solutions.

Warehousing Servicess

Project Cargo

Designing advanced project logistics requires flexibility, specific knowledge and a true passion to find solutions to the challenges that arise. Our people have the skills and the determination to making our team a One-Stop-Shop for your specialized project cargo transports.

No matter the place, the complexity or the dimensions: we can transport any heavy lift or oversized cargo to its final destination. This requires a smart planning from the first stages to avoid extra costs and to manage all the regulative aspects, like taxes or customs compliances.

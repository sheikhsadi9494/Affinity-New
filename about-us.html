About Us

Affinity Trans Logistics is a 100% Neutral International Freight Consolidator providing EXPORT/ IMPORT NVOCC Services to the Freight Forwarding and Customs Broker Industry.

Affinity is backed by a blend of unflinching professionalism operating under strict corporate philosophies, with its vision, values and mission always in mind. Building on this philosophy, Affinity was established with a vision to not only provide reliable services to customers, but to remain neutral and independent – and we stand firm in saying that this is just NOT matched by ANY of our competitors.

From its base in India, Affinity works in line with shipping across the world, always complying with national and international regulations and legislations. Our primary objective is customer satisfaction, and we are always attentive and responsive to any challenges that customers can face. Being a globally operational shipping company, to ensure its ability to compete internationally against the big boys/old guard of the consol business, Affinity has developed key strategic alliances with Consolidation Groups. This allows us to operate with a combined Hub and Spoke Network spanning 139 countries ensuring continued growth.

OUR PRESENCE

Incorporated in 2016, Affinity Trans Logistics Private Limited is established in Chennai, Kolkata and Visakhapatnam ports on the east coast, Cochin, Mumbai, Mundra Ports on the west coast of India along with Ahmedabad, Bangalore and Delhi Inland container depots. We believe in building long-term relationships with our stakeholders. Our local presence in the ports and ICD’s in which we operate gives us an excellent opportunity to use our world-class knowledge and expertise to support customers and partners locally.

Business Code Of Conduct

Business code of conduct

The Affinity Trans Business code of conduct outlines the rights and responsibilities of all Associates and Members of Affinity Trans. By receiving this Business code of conduct, all parties declare to abide by its full contents. Affinity Trans reserves the right to change this Business code of conduct at any time. When amended, such information shall be promptly communicated.

Associates; all business partners of Affinity Trans including customers, vendors, suppliers, agents, subcontractors, or other intermediates or third parties.

Comply with local laws and regulations where business is conducted. Respect human rights, as well as cultural, religious and political customs. Follow environmental conventions and enforce green business policies. Take responsibility for employee safety and well-being. Prioritise and practice corporate social responsibility. Promote compliance with Affinity’s Business code of conduct among its affiliates.

Members; all employees of Affinity Trans, full-time, part time, contract or otherwise, including management, executive members, and stakeholders.

Comply with all applicable and changing laws and legislation.

Abide by local cultural, religious and political customs.

Reject unethical, corrupt, or fraudulent practices.

Shun personal gain through the abuse of our position.

Prohibit and penalise discrimination of any kind.

Serve our customers with their interests at heart.

Compete on fair terms and maintain decorum with competitors.

Honour contracts made with partners and suppliers.

Respect every team member, irrespective of rank.

Create sustainable solutions and reduce our carbon footprint.

Acknowledge and pursue our corporate social responsibilities.

Follow Affinity’s internal policies and practices.

Uphold and maintain Affinity’s quality standards.

Promote Affinity’s Business code of conduct among our members.

Affinity will enforce strict compliance with this Business code of conduct and violations will be investigated. To report violations, please inform us at hr@affinitytrans.com or info@affinitytrans.com. Rest assured, Affinity Trans shall protect the anonymity of the informant while conducting its investigation. Thank you for receiving the Affinity Business code of conduct.
